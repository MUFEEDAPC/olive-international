Explore Saudi Arabia's hidden gems.

Saudi Tourism

Edge of the World

The Edge of the World in Saudi Arabia is a natural escarpment with breathtaking views, located about 96km northwest of Riyadh. It's a popular destination for adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts.

Elephant Rock

Elephant Rock, located in Al Ula, is a natural sandstone formation that resembles an elephant drinking water. It has become a popular tourist destination due to its unique shape and stunning desert scenery, attracting visitors from all over the world.

Al-'Ula

Al-'Ula is an ancient city in the northwestern region of Saudi Arabia. It's home to several archaeological and historical sites, including the famous rock formations, tombs, and temples. It's also a UNESCO World Heritage site.

International Driving License

An International Driving License is a document that allows you to legally drive a vehicle in more than 150 countries worldwide without having to take additional driving tests.
